# FitHub AI - Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Product Name
FitHub AI

## Tagline
The Digital Fitness Hub

## Vision
FitHub AI is a futuristic AI-powered Digital Personal Fitness Trainer that combines home workouts, gym workouts, AI coaching, Digital Twin technology, nutrition intelligence, gym discovery, and body transformation into one immersive platform.

## Mission
Help every person achieve their fitness goals through personalized AI guidance.

## Target Users
- Beginners
- Gym members
- Home workout users
- Athletes
- Busy professionals
- Weight loss users
- Muscle gain users

## Core Features

### AI Coach
Provides personalized workout and nutrition guidance.

### Digital Twin
Displays the user's body as an evolving avatar.

### Home Workouts
Equipment-free and guided workouts.

### Gym Discovery
Find nearby gyms with AI recommendations.

### AI Nutrition
Personalized meal planning and macro tracking.

### Future Projection
Shows estimated body transformation over time.

### Progress Tracking
Track workouts, weight, nutrition, and achievements.
